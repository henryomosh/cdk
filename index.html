    Frequently Asked Questions

    Get answers to your heathcare questions or about our organization from trusted experts

    What makes CDK Healthcare different from other Healthcare Agencies??

    We have been providing care to clients as a locally operated, Medicare Certified, State Licensed Home Health Care Agency. We offer both non-medical and skilled medical care which is supervised by the Ministry of Health.

    What are your office hours?

    We are available in the office Monday thru Friday from 8:30am-5:00pm. A highly trained manager is available during non-business hours to receive your call personally. We are committed to provide 24/7 support to all our clients. We are always just a phone call away!.

    How long will it take to find an appointment with a doctor?

    Every request is different; however, most cases can be staffed the same day or within 24 hours.

    Can you care for someone in a facilit?

    Yes, we can provide service in a hospital, nursing home, assisted living environment, or home.

    Is there a certain number of hours I need to schedule an appointment?

    Yes, we require a 2 hour minimum per shift if that means once a week or once a month. We provide care anywhere from 2-24 hours a day 7 days a week 365 days a year.

    How are your services paid for?

    Personal Health Care accepts Medicare, M-PESA, Medicaid, VA benefits, all PA Waiver programs, Workers Compensation, Long-Term Care Insurance, Private Health Insurance, and Private Pay..
